Super Silly - Button Factory

Projection Mapping / Set Design

Client: Super Silly, Aiken Promotions

Venue: Button Factory, Dublin, IE

As part of a continuing collaboration with Super Silly, the band approached me to create the concept design for the Super Silly show in the Button Factory. We explored the possibility of incorporating a vibrant projection mapped visual design. The visual projection piece is compromised of a 5 foot by 2 foot band logo which spanned the length of the stage in the venue. This piece bathes the viewer. The interplay between the stage lighting and the visual design coaxed the audio inside the visual experience.
