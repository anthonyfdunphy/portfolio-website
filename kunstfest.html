Blutsee - Kunstfest Weimar


TouchDesigner Developer / Sensor Development

Client: Kunstfest Weimar

In the center is a “lake of blood”, surrounded by four loudspeakers from which a string quartet sounds, Beethoven's Great Fugue op. 132. However, only one of the voices of this work can be heard from each of the four loudspeakers. The “sweet spot”, the place where the audience can hear the entire string quartet ideally and clearly, is in the center between the speakers. Outside this “sphere”, level differences and transit times lead to distortions. Only when a listener enters the center does a temperate atmosphere arise. But to do this, the “lake of blood” must be crossed.


Created using an Arudino and a TouchDesigner patch, this platform reacts dynamically as an audience member steps onto its metallic surface. The patch subtly adjusts a once-dissonant audio sample, guiding it back to its harmonious state. As they stand, the melody ascends with clarity and warmth; when they depart, it gracefully recedes. This immersive experience underscores the delicate dance between technology and artistry, making each individual not just an observer but an essential part of the unfolding narrative.
