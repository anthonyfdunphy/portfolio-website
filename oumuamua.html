OUMUAMUA

TouchDesigner Developer / Sensor Development

Client: Marc Sinan Company

Venue: Spreehalle, Berlin, DE

Spatial audio, visual and lighting installation in Berlin which harnessed the capabilities of TouchDesigner to craft interactive lighting and mesmerizing visual effects. The essence of the project resonated with a blend of technical prowess and artistic vision, mirroring a seamless interplay of dynamic elements. Beyond the visual spectacle, I orchestrated a comprehensive show control system, this intricate setup seamlessly integrated and synchronized Resolume for control of playback of projection mapped video material and Ableton - all achieved with OSC control.
