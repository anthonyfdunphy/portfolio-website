Yamamori Tengu

Creative Direction / Lighting Design / Visual Content / 2018

Client: Yamamori Tengu

Venue: Tengu / Kaizen Bar

1

I was approached by management at Yamamori Tengu - Stran Street Great, North City, Dublin 1 - to create a lighting design for both the downstairs Tengu bar and the upstairs venue, the Kaizan bar. The brief was to create a standalone lighting solution that coule be adapted to suit promoters and artists for techno, disco and other club nights. Programming began of the various moving head fixtures, LED par cans and LED strip fixtures. Different scenes were created and stored in software that trasmitted the various scenes to the lighting universe. Full control was given to allow the management to make adjustments to colours in the room or position of individual fixtures.
